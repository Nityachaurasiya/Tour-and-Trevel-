Frequently Asked Questions

How to reach Ranthambore by train from Jaipur?

The nearest railway station to Ranthambore National Park is Sawai Madhopur Railway Station, which is located almost 13.8 km away. After arriving, you can catch a bus or taxi to take you to the park. The entire drive will take you almost 31 minutes. There are 33 trains that run between Jaipur to Sawai Madhopur, including daily trains.

What is the distance between Jaipur to Ranthambore?

The distance from Jaipur to Ranthambore is around 190 km.

Is there no direct flight connectivity from Jaipur to Ranthambore?

Jaipur International Airport is itself the nearest airport to Ranthambore National Park. After landing, you will have to hire a taxi or private vehicle.

Is there any direct train connectivity from Jaipur to Ranthambore?

No, there is no direct train connectivity from Jaipur to Ranthambore. Sawai Madhopur is the nearest railway station to Ranthambore National Park.

Is there any direct bus/cab connectivity from Jaipur to Ranthambore?

No, there are no direct buses from Jaipur to Ranthambore. You can hire a private vehicle as a cab to reach Ranthambore National Park from Jaipur.

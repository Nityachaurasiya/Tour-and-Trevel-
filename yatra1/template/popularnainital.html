Overview

Welcome to the breathtaking hill station of Nainital in Uttarakhand, where love is in the air and nature is all around you. Bon Travel India invites you to embark on a romantic journey with our 4 nights 5 days Nainital honeymoon packages that offer the perfect blend of adventure, relaxation, and exploration. Nainital, nestled in the heart of the Uttarakhand Himalayas, is a charming hill station filled with stunning landscapes, beautiful lakes, and vibrant flora and fauna. From the majestic Himalayas to the serene Naini Lake, there is a lot to see and experience in Nainital. Our Nainital honeymoon itinerary includes a sightseeing tour of the town, as well as an excursion tour to Bhimtal, Naukuchiatal, and Sattal, where you can explore the wonders of nature with your better half. Get ready to indulge in some romantic moments as you trek to Tiffin Top and visit the Snow View Point to take in the breathtaking views of the surrounding mountains. Enjoy a serene boat ride on Naini Lake and shop to your heart’s content at Mall Road. Our 4 nights 5 days Nainital honeymoon tour package promises to create lasting memories for you and your partner, whether you’re exploring the town’s natural beauty or enjoying each other’s company in the comfort of your hotel room. With Bon Travel India, you can be assured of a memorable Nainital honeymoon tour package that caters to all your needs and desires.

Here is the short itinerary for 4 Nights 5 Days Nainital Honeymoon Delight

Day 01:

Delhi – Nainital (305 km/ 7 hrs) 
Your adventure begins with a scenic drive from Delhi to Nainital.
Upon arriving in Nainital, check-in at the hotel.
The rest of the evening is yours to explore the charming town at your leisure or relax in the comfort of your hotel room.
Enjoy an overnight stay and dinner at the hotel in Nainital

Day 02:

Nainital Sightseeing Tour 
After breakfast in the morning, get ready for a romantic sightseeing tour of Nainital.
Enjoy boating with your partner on Naini Lake and visit popular tourist attractions in Nainital, including Bhimtal, Naukuchiatal, and Sattal.
After a day of exploration, head back to the hotel for a delicious dinner and an overnight stay

Day 03:

Nainital – Ranikhet – Nainital 
Today, after breakfast, get ready for an exciting road trip to Ranikhet.
Ranikhet is a small hill paradise blessed with verdant meadows, trees, and colorful flowers.
On reaching Ranikhet, visit the Ranikhet Golf Course, apple orchards, Jhula Devi Temple, and Majhkhali.
Spend some quality time together at Kalika and Bhalu Dam before heading back to Nainital for an overnight stay.

Day 04:

Full day trip to Nainital 
Day 4 is packed with exciting activities that promise to create lasting memories.
Start the day with a trek to Tiffin Top and take in the stunning views of the surrounding mountains.
Next, visit the Snow View Point, where you can indulge in some moments of privacy and romance with your partner.
Enjoy a serene boat ride on the iconic Naini Lake and indulge in some shopping at Mall Road.
The rest of the day is yours to enjoy at your leisure.
Overnight stay and dinner at the hotel in Nainital

Day 05:

Nainital – Delhi 
Your Nainital honeymoon package comes to an end on day 5.
After breakfast, check out from the hotel and travel comfortably by road back to Delhi.
Catch your flight or train back home with fond memories of your romantic getaway in Nainital.

Here is the short tour itinerary for a 7 nights 8 days family holiday in Andaman-

Upon your arrival in Andaman, you will be warmly greeted by a representative from Bon Travel India. They will escort you to your
hotel in Port Blair, ensuring a comfortable start to your journey.
After refreshing yourself, head to the Phoenix Bay Jetty for a delightful boat ride to Ross Island. This small and picturesque 
island is a treasure trove of preserved heritage, and the 20-minute ferry ride from Port Blair offers spectacular views.
Once you’ve explored Ross Island, proceed to North Bay Island, where you can witness the magnificent coral reefs up close through 
activities such as snorkeling, sea walking, or scuba diving.
Enjoy thrilling experiences like high-speed boating, glass-bottom boating, dolphin boat cruises, and deep-sea adventures.
Conclude the day with a visit to the intriguing Viper Island, known for its historical buildings, gallows where prisoners were executed, and lush greenery.
Enjoy a mesmerizing sunset view from the island before returning to Port Blair and your hotel for the night

Day 01:

Arrival at Port Blair + Ross / Coral/North Bay & Viper Island Excursion 
After a refreshing breakfast, head to the Port Blair Phoenix Jetty to catch a ferry to Neil Island.
The ferry journey spans approximately 40 kilometers and takes 2.5 to 4 hours, depending on the speed of the ferry.
As you glide through the calm seas on a high-speed catamaran, you’ll be treated to a pleasant, breezy ride, surrounded 
by the refreshing scent of the salty ocean air.
Overnight stay and dinner at hotel/resort.

Day 02:

Port Blair to Neil Island 
Start your day by visiting the beautiful natural rock formation known as Howrah Bridge. 
This unique spot offers a chance to observe various species of starfish, 
corals, mollusks, snakes, and sea cucumbers. Please note that the approach to 
the bridge-like rock can be rough, rocky, muddy, and slippery.
Next, visit the exotic Bharatpur Beach, renowned for its crystal-clear blue waters. 
Take a refreshing swim, relax lazily on the beach, or play among the foamy waves.
Later, explore the gentle and sandy Laxmanpur Beach, which exudes sheer charm.
Witness the enchanting golden sunset before returning to your hotel on Neil Island.

Day 03:

Neil Island Excursion 
Start your day with an early morning sunrise experience near your hotel. 
Enjoy the serene beauty and capture breathtaking moments.
After breakfast, pack up and proceed to the Neil Island Jetty to catch a ferry to the stunning Havelock Island.
Upon reaching Havelock, spend the day exploring the island at your own pace. 
Walk along the nearest beach, reveling in the awe-inspiring beauty of the blue Andaman Seas.
Spend a comfortable night at hotel in Havelock

Day 04:

Neil Island to Havelock Island 
Following breakfast, embark on an exciting adventure to Elephant Beach, famous for scuba diving and snorkeling.
Immerse yourself in the underwater world as you explore the vibrant coral reefs during a scuba or snorkeling session.
Alternatively, you can enjoy a glass-bottom boat ride to witness the mesmerizing marine ecosystem.
In the afternoon, proceed to the renowned Radhanagar Beach, consistently ranked as one of the world’s best beaches.
Lose yourself in its serene beauty, enjoying the exquisite views of the sea. Create unforgettable memories to cherish.
Return to your hotel in Havelock for an overnight stay.

Day 05:

Elephant Beach with Snorkeling & Radha Nagar Beach Excursion 
After an early breakfast, embark on a scenic drive to Kalapatthar Beach. Admire the beach’s beauty, characterized by submerged black rocks. 
Although not ideal for swimming or water activities, the beach offers tranquility and a sprawling sea view.
Enjoy the seclusion of Kalapatthar Beach, relishing moments of peace and solitude amidst nature’s splendor.
Later, return to your hotel in Havelock and prepare for the journey back to Port Blair.
Spend the night at your Port Blair hotel, reminiscing about the unforgettable experiences of your trip.

Day 06:

Kalapathar Beach Excursion / Havelock to Port Blair 
Today, embark on an excursion to Mount Harriet National Park, accessible from Port Blair via Bombooflot. 
You can choose to travel by ferry service or by road, with the ferry journey taking approximately 15 to 20 minutes, 
while the road journey covers 40 kilometers in about an hour.
Mount Harriet National Park is spread across 4,662 hectares and is surrounded by majestic mountain ranges. 
Explore the park’s steep 
slopes, rocky beaches, sandy areas, and pristine streams flowing into the sea. Immerse yourself in the 
untouched and unspoiled beauty of this natural paradise.
After a day well spent, return to your hotel in Port Blair for a comfortable night’s stay.

Day 07:

Excursion to Mount Harriet 
Today, embark on an excursion to Mount Harriet National Park, accessible from Port Blair via Bombooflot. 
You can choose to travel by ferry service or by road, 
with the ferry journey taking approximately 15 to 20 minutes, while the road journey covers 40 kilometers in about an hour.
Mount Harriet National Park is spread across 4,662 hectares and is surrounded by majestic mountain ranges. 
Explore the park’s steep slopes, rocky beaches, 
sandy areas, and pristine streams flowing into the sea. Immerse yourself in the untouched and unspoiled beauty of this natural paradise.
After a day well spent, return to your hotel in Port Blair for a comfortable night’s stay.

Day 08:

Departure 
As you depart from the Andaman Islands, filled with unforgettable memories of this island paradise, make your way to the Veer Savarkar Airport.
Carry these cherished memories with you long after you have bid farewell to this remote island beauty.
Your Andaman family vacation trip ends here with us, leaving you with a heart full of happiness and beautiful memories to treasure.
